I Feel Like My Painting/Lamp Experiment

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Initial idea: A lamp that responds to moods/emotions (in an attempt to improve mental health) by measuring heart rates, and displays a form of 'light painting'.

Current idea: Experimental lamp to test how emotions are affected by colors of LED light. Uses two extreme ends of different colors, mapped based on heart rate measurements.
